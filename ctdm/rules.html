General Rules usually these are common sense

1. The use of clientside Lua, client injections, hacks, or exploits to enhance or cheat standard gameplay will not be tolerated. no cheats!

Punishment: The player that is in violation of this rule will receive a Permanent ban.
Note: The use of keyboard/mouse macros will be allowed.

2. Do not advertise for another gaming community, and/or game server in this server. no one likes ads

Punishment: The player that is in violation of this rule will receive a Kick.
3+ Offenses: The player that is has violated this rule on 3 seperate occasions will receive a 3-day ban.

3. You are expected to have common sense when playing on this server. please refer to the General Rules subtitle

Punishment: A very big, slap to the face.

4. Your name must contain at least 3 English, alphanumeric letters. 私の名前は...

On Violation: The player that is in violation of this rule will be asked to modify his name to fit the requirements of this rule.
On Refusal: If the player refused the request, the player will receive a Kick.
2+ Offenses: The player will receive a 2-hour ban.
3+ Offenses: The player will receive a 2-week ban.
4+ Offenses: The player will receive a Permanent ban.

Gameplay the actual stuff

1. The use of "map exploits" or "exploited position" will be allowed, as long as they fit they fit certain criteria. this probably is the longest rule

A player is not violating this rule if they are:
  1. The player in the exploited position must be visible from another player in a non-exploited position at all times.
  2. The player in the exploited position must be eligible to receive damage from another player or non-player spawned entity at all times.
A "map exploit" or "exploited position" is defined as such when:
  1. A player is able to get to a position due to a gameplay mechanic, for example Double Jump, and cannot be accessed without the assistive gameplay mechanic.
  2. The mapper/staff did not intend players to be in that position under normal gameplay circumstances.
  3. A player has any vision of a Nodraw tool texture (https://developer.valvesoftware.com/wiki/Tool_textures, see "Nodraw").
Punishment: If the player has inflicted death on another player in an illegal exploited position, they will receive a Slay, and will be requested to not access that position again.
2+ Offenses: The player will receive a Kick.
3+ Offenses: The player will receive a non-permanent ban of a staffs intended length.

2. Team killing will not be tolerated. you guys will find a way...

Punishment: The player that is in violation of this rule will receive a warning.
5+ Offenses: The player will receive a 1-hour ban.

3. The use of another account or player to get a competitive advantage in normal gameplay circumstances will not be tolerated. ooooOOOOOoooooooo...

Punishment: The player or the assistant player will receive a 7-day ban.
2+ Offenses: Both players will receive a 7-day ban.

4. Prop abuse will not be tolerated. garry's mod props are 10 out of 10

Punishment: The player that is in violation of this rule will receive a Kick.
2+ Offenses: The player will receive a 1-day ban.
3+ Offenses: The player will receive a Permanent ban.
