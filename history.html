2013 the start of something new

AfterMath Revolution is born it's a boy!

June 29th, 2013 is the official date AfterMath Revolution was created. Actually, it's the date that the forums were put up and the actual date of creation of the first server is unknown. The first server ever created under AfterMath's name was a Trouble in Terrorist Town server, which was very popular at the time. The person under this all is The Great White North, or "North" for short.
